Description

Use the GNOME_PAD macros to make GNOME spacing and distances more consistent. GNOME_YES and GNOME_NO, GNOME_OK and GNOME_CANCEL are used to represent button numbers in yes-no or ok-cancel GnomeDialogs. Ignore the GNOME_KEY_ macros, they are old cruft.
