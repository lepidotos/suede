Using GHex

When starting GHex, it will pop up a window with nothing but a menubar.

Figure 1. GHex Main Window

When a file is loaded, its contents will be displayed below the menubar in an area that will occupy the largest part of the window. The contents are displayed in both hex and ascii at once, with hexadecimal values on the left and the corresponding ascii representation of those bytes on the right-hand side of the display.

Figure 2. GHex Main Window
